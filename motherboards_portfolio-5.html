Motherboards

What is a motherboard?

A motherboard is a circuit board inside generalpurpose computing systems, including personal computers, smart televisions, smart monitors, and other similar devices, which supports communication between different electrical components and houses components such as the CPU, memory, etc.

(Spiceworks.com , 2024)

Different Types of Motherboards

  • Advanced Technology (AT) motherboard
  • Standard ATX motherboard
  • Micro ATX motherboard
  • eXtended ATX motherboard
  • Flex ATX motherboard
  • Low-Profile EXtended (LPX) motherboard
  • BTX motherboard
  • Pico BTX motherboard
  • Mini ITX motherboard
  • Mini STX motherboard

Kinds of Motherboards

Form Factor Build CPU Slots Memory Slots Chipsets BIOS PCI Slots SATA Built-in Features
AT MOTHERBOARD Early personal computers 1 CPU Socket 2-4 SIMM slots 1-2 (Northbridge and Southbridge) 1 BIOS Chip 5-8 ISA slots - Supports early CPUs like the Intel 80286 and 80386.
STANDARD ATX MOTHERBOARD General-purpose desktop computers 1 CPU Socket 2-4 DIMM slots 2 (Northbridge and Southbridge) 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 5-7 (PCI, PCIe) 4-8 SATA ports Built-in network connectivity
MICRO ATX MOTHERBOARD Compact desktops for home or office use. 1 CPU Socket 2-4 DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 3-4 (PCI, PCIe) 4-6 SATA ports Many models come with onboard video, useful for basic display needs.
EXTENDED ATX MOTHERBOARD Servers and professional workstations. 1-2 (supports dual CPUs) 6-8 DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 7-8 (PCIe) 6-10 SATA ports Supports larger amounts of RAM, often 8 or more slots.
FLEX ATX MOTHERBOARD Entry-level desktops 1 CPU Socket 2 DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 2-3 (PCI, PCIe) 2-4 SATA ports Suitable for users with minimal hardware needs
LOW-PROFILE EXTENDED (LPX) MOTHERBOARD Early home theater PCs (HTPCs). 1 CPU Socket 2-4 SIMM/ DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS Chip Uses a riser card (adds 2-3 slots) - Fits in low-profile desktop enclosures.
BTX MOTHERBOARD High-performance PCs needing improved thermal management. 1 CPU Socket 2 DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 4-7 (PCIe) 4-8 SATA ports Components arranged to enhance cooling efficiency
PICO BTX MOTHERBOARD Compact desktops with higher thermal demands. 1 CPU Socket 2-4 DIMM slots 2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 1-2 (limited due to size) 2-4 SATA ports Better cooling than comparable small form factors.
MINI ITX MOTHERBOARD Embedded systems and appliances. 1 (sometimes soldered CPU) 1-2 DIMM slots 1-2 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip 1 (PCIe x16) 2-4 SATA ports Gigabit Ethernet, with some models offering Wi-Fi and Bluetooth.
MINI STX MOTHERBOARD Ultra-compact desktops and mini PCs 1 CPU Socket 2 SO-DIMM slots 1 1 BIOS/ UIEF Chip None (supports M.2 slots) M.2 slots, possibly 1 SATA port Perfect for basic computing tasks in a minimal footprint.
